Product Details

Our premium Burgundy Peruvian Straight wig is made from 100% virgin Peruvian human hair with rich burgundy red tones. The pre-plucked hairline with bleached knots creates a natural appearance that mimics a real scalp.

Key Features:

  • Material: 100% Peruvian Human Hair
  • Lace: HD Transparent 13x4 Lace Front
  • Length: 16"-24" (customizable)
  • Density: 180%-200%
  • Heat Friendly: Can be styled with heat tools up to 350°F
  • Included: Free wig cap, adjustable straps, and baby hairs

Care Instructions:

  1. Wash with sulfate-free shampoo
  2. Condition regularly
